Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken with Roasted Mediterranean Vegetables

Servings:
4
Prep Time:
30 minutes (plus 2 hours marinating)
Cook Time:
45 minutes

Ingredients

For the Chicken Marinade

  • 4 chicken breasts (boneless, skinless)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Juice and zest of 2 lemons
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh oregano, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

For the Roasted Vegetables

  • 2 red bell peppers, chunked
  • 1 large zucchini, sliced
  • 1 large yellow squash, sliced
  • 1 red onion, wedged
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 1 head garlic, top sliced off
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons dried herbs de Provence
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Herb Sauce

  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon capers, chopped
  • 1 small lemon, juiced
  • 1 small shallot, finely minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    • In a large bowl, combine all marinade ingredients
    • Add chicken breasts and coat well
    • Cover and refrigerate for 2-4 hours
    • Remove from refrigerator 30 minutes before cooking
  2. Prepare the Vegetables:
    • Preheat oven to 400째F (200째C)
    • Toss prepared vegetables with olive oil, balsamic vinegar, and herbs
    • Spread on a large baking sheet, leaving space between pieces
    • Place the garlic head in the center, drizzled with oil
    • Season with salt and pepper
  3. Roast the Vegetables:
    • Roast for 25-30 minutes
    • Stir halfway through cooking
    • Vegetables should be tender and lightly charred
    • Remove garlic head when soft and squeeze out cloves
  4. Grill the Chicken:
    • Preheat grill to medium-high heat
    • Remove chicken from marinade
    • Grill 6-7 minutes per side until internal temperature reaches 165째F (74째C)
    • Let rest for 5 minutes before slicing
  5. Make the Herb Sauce:
    • Combine all sauce ingredients in a bowl
    • Stir well and let sit for 10 minutes to meld flavors
    • Adjust seasoning to taste
  6. Serve:
    • Slice chicken against the grain
    • Arrange vegetables on a platter
    • Place sliced chicken on top
    • Drizzle with herb sauce
    • Garnish with extra fresh herbs if desired

Notes

  • For best results, let chicken marinate overnight
  • You can use dried herbs in the marinade (use 1/3 of the fresh amount)
  • Vegetable choices can be seasonal - use what's fresh and available
  • The herb sauce can be made ahead and stored for up to 24 hours
  • Perfect for meal prep - vegetables can be roasted in advance

Storage

  • Store leftover chicken and vegetables separately in airtight containers
  • Keeps well in refrigerator for up to 3 days
  • Herb sauce stays fresh for 2 days when refrigerated
